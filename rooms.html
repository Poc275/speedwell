Our Rooms

Speedwell House has two ensuite double bedrooms (with showers not baths). Along with beautiful views of Peveril Castle, the rooms are furnished to a very high standard with flat screen HD TVs, tea and coffee making facilities, hair driers, and free Wi-Fi.

Our bedrooms have been individually designed to ensure you have a relaxing and comfortable stay.

Regrettably we do not allow pets in the house, however we will soon be developing our barns as self-catering holiday accommodation and these will be dog friendly. The house is completely non-smoking.
