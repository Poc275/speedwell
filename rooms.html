Our Rooms

Speedwell House has two ensuite double bedrooms (with showers not baths). Along with beautiful views of Peveril Castle, the rooms are furnished to a high standard with flat screen TVs, tea and coffee making facilities, toiletries, hair driers, and free Wi-Fi.

We’re positive you’ll enjoy your stay with us and we can’t wait to start looking after you, but before we can we need to tell you about our T&Cs:

Minimum stay at weekends is 2 nights (same occupants). For bank holidays this is 3 nights (also same occupants).

If you need single night occupancy please check with us as this may be available.

Sorry we do not allow children under 12 to stay at Speedwell House. Regrettably we do not allow pets in the house. The house is completely non-smoking.

Arrivals are after 3pm (unless previously arranged). The latest arrival is 9pm. Check out is at 11am.

Payment on departure by either cash or card.
