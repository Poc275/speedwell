Walks:

  • Trek up to Mam Tor and walk the ridge to Lose Hill, the entire loop can be viewed from the rear of Speedwell House
  • Walk along Peakshole Water from Speedwell House into the village and experience its quirky and individual shops including Blue John Jewellery and melt in the mouth traditional fudge at the Real Fudge Company
  • There is also a variety of other walks from relaxing strolls to demanding climbs in the Dark Peak Gritstone to the North and the White Peak Limestone to the South
