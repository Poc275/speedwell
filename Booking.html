Our Rates

Weekend and Bank Holiday Rates:
£85.00 per night, minimum stay 2 nights.

Sunday to Thursday Rates:
£75.00 per night, no minimum stay.

Arrivals are after 3pm (unless previously arranged). The latest arrival is 9pm. Check out is at 11am.

We do not have any family rooms and are therefore unable to accommodate babies and children under the age of 12.

All prices are per room per night and include a breakfast of your choice.

If you need single night occupancy please check with us as this may be available.

Please do not hesitate to contact us near to your required date to check last minute availability and deals.

Deposit: Non-refundable £20.00 per room per night payable by bank transfer.

Cancellation Policy: 14 Days. If cancelled within 14 days of arrival date, the full room costs are chargeable.

We are closed over Christmas but we are open over the New Year period.
