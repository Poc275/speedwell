Our Rooms

Speedwell House has two ensuite double bedrooms (with showers not baths) both these rooms have views of Peveril Castle, the rooms are furnished to a high standard with flat screen TV's, hair dryer, tea and coffee making facilities, local toiletries and free wifi.

Minimum stay at weekends is 2 nights (same occupants). Bank holidays 3 nights stay (same occupants). Please check single night occupancy as this may be available.

Sorry we do not allow children under 12 to stay at Speedwell House. Regrettably we do not allow pets in the house. The house is completely non-smoking.

Arrivals after 3.00 pm (unless previously arranged) latest arrival is 9pm. Check out by 11 am. Payment on departure by cash or card.
