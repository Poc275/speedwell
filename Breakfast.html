Breakfast

Breakfast is served between 8 and 10am in our bright and spacious breakfast room which is on the ground floor.

Our delicious breakfasts will set you up for the day whether you choose a full English breakfast or one of our lighter choices. Most dietary requirements can be catered for with prior notice.

There is also a choice of cereals, granola, yoghurts and fruit along with toast and preserves.
