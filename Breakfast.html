Breakfast

Breakfast is served between 7.30 and 9.00 am in our bright and spacious breakfast room which is on the ground floor.

Our delicious breakfasts will set you up for the day whether you choose a full Cumberland breakfast or one of our vegetarian or lighter options. Special diets can be catered for and locally sourced produce is served whenever possible.

Packed lunches, cream tea's and homemade soups are available on request and at an additional cost.
